Explore Liver Fibrosis Stages

Stage F0: No Fibrosis

The liver is healthy with no scarring.

Stage F1: Mild Fibrosis

Minimal scarring with no significant damage.

Stage F2: Moderate Fibrosis

Increased fibrosis, but the liver still functions well.

Stage F3: Severe Fibrosis

Extensive scarring; high risk of liver failure.

Stage F4: Cirrhosis

Severe liver damage, leading to possible liver failure.
